# Changelog

## 1.0.0

- Initial release of the SecondaryAttacks configurable weapon system.
- Added ranged presets for barrage, volley, piercing, scatter, spiral, sentinel, meteor, burst, sticky bombs, and overcharged bombs.
- Added melee presets for sneak ambush, rift trails, cleaving thrusts, launch slams, aftershocks, knockback chains, boomerangs, impact bursts, spear rain, spinning sweeps, fracture lines, and harvest sweeps.
- Added Blood Magic summon empower, StaffShield conversion, summon quality scaling, and summon prefab override support.
- Added ServerSync YAML configuration, cooldown HUD support, localization hooks, and WarfareTweaks bridge compatibility.
